## Our Differentiation Strategy

### What We DON'T Do (vs Competitors)
- ❌ No hardware requirements (unlike Toast POS, Square)
- ❌ No delivery/takeaway (unlike Glovo, Deliveroo, UberEats)
- ❌ No complex POS integration (keeping it simple)

### What We DO
- ✅ Pure web-based QR menu (scan and browse)
- ✅ Dine-in ordering at table
- ✅ Focus on customer experience enhancements
- ✅ Multi-language support (VN/EN priority, IT for expansion)
- ✅ Multi-currency support
- ✅ Customization & preferences system
- ✅ WiFi quick connect

## Strategic Insights

### Why Vietnam First?
- Growing digital adoption in F&B sector
- Tourist-heavy cities (Da Nang, HCMC, Hanoi)
- Need for multi-language menus (VN/EN essential)
- Less saturated market than US/Europe
- Lower customer acquisition cost

### Critical Success Factors
1. **Speed:** Fast load times on Vietnam's mobile networks
2. **Simplicity:** No training required for staff or customers
3. **Reliability:** Works offline or with poor connectivity
4. **Localization:** Perfect Vietnamese translations
5. **Mobile-First:** 95%+ of users scan QR from mobile
