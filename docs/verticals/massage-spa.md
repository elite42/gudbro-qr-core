# Massage/Spa Vertical

## Industry Overview

### **Market Size**
- **Vietnam:** ~15,000 massage/spa businesses
- **Da Nang:** ~300 businesses (high tourist + local demand)
- **Average daily clients:** 10-30 per business
- **Market segments:** Budget ($5-15), Mid-range ($15-40), Luxury ($40-100+)

### **Current Pain Points**
1. **Phone-only booking** (missed calls = lost revenue)
2. **No online presence** (just Facebook/Google listing)
3. **Language barriers** (tourists can't communicate needs)
4. **Paper price menus** (outdated, unprofessional)
5. **No therapist selection** (customers want to choose)
6. **No package deals** (no incentive for repeat visits)
7. **Cash-only payment** (inconvenient for tourists)
8. **No customer database** (can't do marketing)

---

## Market Examples

### **Typical Massage Shop in Da Nang/Vietnam**
- Small storefront (3-8 treatment rooms)
- Basic signboard with services + prices
- Staff standing outside inviting tourists
- Handwritten menu boards
- WhatsApp/Zalo for bookings
- Commission-based therapists

**Average Pricing (Da Nang):**
- Foot massage (45 min): 100k-150k VND ($4-6)
- Body massage (60 min): 150k-250k VND ($6-10)
- Oil massage (90 min): 250k-400k VND ($10-16)
- Hot stone massage: 400k-600k VND ($16-24)
